AI is modeled as a subclass of character.
Each armored core is operated by a character.
The schema records a society name as a literal value.
Each character stores a buddy flag as a literal value.
The schema records that a boss is boss of a major event.
Each character can be affiliated with a society.
The schema records that an entity is hostile toward an entity.
A character kills another character.
Each major event stores its timeline position as a literal value.
A society records whether it originates on Rubicon as a literal value.
